With a zest of creating potential 21st-century Entrepreneurs, DABOTICS intends to promote STEM Education for K – 12 student and Engineering Students. STEM Education is a curriculum to educate students in four domains of study – science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Since this form of education fosters critical & innovative thinking, scientific, and analytical literacy, we understand STEM education’s importance and provide students with exclusive STEM LABS, where they can be trained in technical education and can upgrade their skills in different upgrowing fields like Robotics, Engineering, Computer Science, AI etc with practical experience and in-hand activities and exposure. DABOTICS was founded in NOIDA in the year 2019 which provides Innovation labs and workshops for schools and colleges across India. DABOTICS has conducted workshops for 100k+ students in India. In 2019, the first STEM & ROBOTICS lab of NORTH INDIA was inaugurated at Noida. DABOTICS in partnership with RAJSHREEE GROUP conducted a STEM workshop for 100+ schools across 24 Districts in UTTAR PRADESH.
